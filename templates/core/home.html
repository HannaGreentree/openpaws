Do you care about the impact of your donations? Would you prefer to donate and then see images of stray cats that have just eaten the food you provided, or a happy dog whose surgery was made possible because of your support? Or would you rather donate and never know how your money was used?

We live in a highly technological world where almost everything is traceable. Social media plays a major role in our daily lives — we can see what people do, what they eat, where they shop, and even where they travel. Some people even donate to bloggers to support the content they create. However, when it comes to donating to non-profit organisations or individuals who claim to help animals, transparency is often limited. It can be difficult to verify how donations are used or whether the information provided is accurate.

Think about a time when you wanted to donate but decided not to. Perhaps you felt overwhelmed by the number of organisations online or unsure which one to trust. Why do some large, well-known charity organisations appear to spend money on expensive events or have high operational costs? Why is it sometimes difficult to find clear financial information? Why do some social media profiles have thousands of followers but very little real engagement? These questions can make it difficult to identify truly reliable organisations or individuals to support. As a result, some people choose not to donate at all — which means that genuine cases and animals in need are left without support. The OpenPaws platform was created to change that.

Every time people donate money, they rely on trust. Often, donors only know what organisations choose to share with them. We are not here to judge organisations — we are here to make the donation process more transparent and give donors the opportunity to clearly see how their money is being used. OpenPaws is designed to track donations and highlight trustworthy non-profit organisations and individuals who genuinely deserve your support. Instead of searching on the internet for a trustworthy volunteer or organisation, you can simply visit OpenPaws and see all the animal supporters registered with us, how many cases each of them has successfully closed, and how many positive points they have. You can also see whether they have any negative points, which indicates that they failed to provide proof of how they used the funds.

FIVE STEPS OF TRANSPARENCY

1

Shelters Create Cases

Animal shelters and volunteers create cases describing the support they need, such as food, veterinary treatment, parasite prevention, or other essential expenses.

2

Donors Support the Platform

Visitors can donate to support the vital work of shelters and volunteers, helping to provide essential care for animals in need.

3

Admin Reviews Requests

Each request is reviewed by an admin before funding is approved. A case can only be considered if sufficient funds are available in the platform balance.

4

Shelters Provide Proof

Once funds are used, shelters must upload proof, such as a link to a social media post where everyone can view receipts, purchased items, and photos of the animals they have helped.

5

Shelters Earn Points

Once a case is successfully closed, the shelter receives one positive point. If a shelter fails to provide proof of how the funds were used, it receives one negative point.

HOW OPENPAWS WORKS

There is no doubt that some organisations and individuals genuinely want to help animals and dedicate themselves to this work. We believe that, for those who are truly committed to animal welfare, providing transparency should not be a problem. If you already support animals in need, that is great. The next time you see someone asking for donations, invite them to join OpenPaws. Once they are registered with us, you can support them and track your donation.

If one of them fails to provide proof of how the funds were used, this will be visible to everyone else and reflected automatically in the organisation’s or individual’s record on the platform. This helps donors make informed decisions and easily identify trustworthy organisations and individuals. The purpose of OpenPaws is to bring together reliable and trustworthy animal volunteers, shelters, and charities that are willing to show their “open paws” and share clear, honest information about their work.

WHY TRANSPARENCY MATTERS

Donors deserve to know how their money is used. That’s why OpenPaws ensures transparency by requiring shelters to provide proof of spending before cases are closed. We believe that our platform could attract more donors and encourage more people to support animals in need. This creates trust between donors and shelters and encourages the responsible use of donations.

NO FEE

OpenPaws is registered in the United Kingdom as a non-profit organisation, which means that every £10 donated is directed fully to the selected case. We are an independent platform operated by a technical team of volunteers. We do not represent any specific organisation. Our role is to provide a transparent system for tracking donations and expenses. Our goal is to inspire more people to donate by providing clear, accessible information about how every pound is used.
